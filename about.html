About Our Platform

Our Mission

The Forex Fundamental Analysis Dashboard was created to provide traders, investors, and financial professionals with institutional-grade fundamental analysis tools in an accessible, user-friendly format.

We believe that understanding the fundamental drivers of currency movements—economic growth, monetary policy, inflation, and trade dynamics—is essential for making informed trading decisions in the foreign exchange market.

What We Provide

Our platform offers comprehensive fundamental analysis across major G10 currencies:

  • Real-Time FX Rates: Live exchange rates updated every 60 seconds
  • Economic Indicators Heatmap: Visual representation of GDP growth, inflation, unemployment, interest rates, and more
  • Currency Strength Scoring: Quantitative assessment (0-100 scale) based on fundamental factors
  • Central Bank Monitoring: Track policy rates, meeting schedules, and monetary policy outlook
  • Trade & Current Account Data: Monitor external balance positions and competitiveness
  • Sentiment Classification: Systematic categorization into bullish, bearish, or neutral outlooks

Our Methodology

The fundamental strength scoring system employs a multi-factor quantitative model that evaluates:

Factor Weights

  • Monetary Policy Stance (25%): Interest rate differentials, central bank forward guidance, real interest rates
  • External Balance (25%): Current account position, trade balance, capital flows
  • Growth Dynamics (20%): GDP growth relative to trend, industrial production, economic momentum
  • Price Stability (15%): Inflation deviation from target, core inflation trends, inflation expectations
  • Labor Market (15%): Unemployment versus natural rate, employment growth, wage pressures
  • Fiscal Position (10%): Government debt-to-GDP, fiscal deficit, credit ratings

Scores are dynamically weighted based on market regime, volatility conditions, and relative importance of factors in the current macroeconomic environment.
