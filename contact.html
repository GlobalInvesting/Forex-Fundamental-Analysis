Frequently Asked Questions

How often is the data updated?

Foreign exchange rates are updated every 60 seconds. Economic indicators are refreshed every 24 hours, and central bank data is updated following official announcements.

Is this financial advice?

No. Our platform provides informational and educational content only. We do not provide financial advice, investment recommendations, or trading signals. Please see our Terms of Service for more details.

What currencies do you cover?

We currently cover eight major currencies: USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, AUD, CAD, CHF, and NZD. Additional currencies may be added in future updates.

Do you offer an API?
